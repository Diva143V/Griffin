## 🎯 Objective & Rationale
To validate the hypothesis that concomitant administration of Metformin and a Pan-HER inhibitor (Neratinib) induces superior cytotoxicity in HER2-positive breast cancer models compared to monotherapy. 
This experiment specifically aims to:
1.  Quantify cell viability reduction using CellTiter-Glo® luminescence assay after **48-hour exposure** post-treatment setup.
2.  Assess downregulation of canonical survival nodes (ERK, AKT, mTOR) via Western Blot at time-point +72h.
3.  Verify cytotoxic thresholds established by positive controls (Doxorubicin).
